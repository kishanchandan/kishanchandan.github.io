Some of our exciting demonstrations are available to try online. The live demonstration below allows you to teleoperate a virtual human to work alongside a team of robots in a simulated warehouse environment. You can use your mouse to look around, and the W, A, S, and, D keys to move in the environment.

Go on help the robots, they might be waiting at one of the four drop stations for you to help them. Hint: Visiting a drop station automatically unloads the box! Hurray! You are now qualified to help the robots.
